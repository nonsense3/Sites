sunburn

Sunburn Festival is one of the biggest commercial electronic dance music festivals in Asia and is held in Goa , INDIA.This Festival held at the end of December every year.From its inception in 2007 until about 2015 the Sunburn venue was in Vagator Beach, but in 2016 Sunburn Festival moved to Pune. Finally, it made its way back to Goa again in 2019.Many Dj s come from another country to GOA like- 'The Chainsmokers' , 'Martin Garrix' , 'DJ Snake' , 'KSHMR' , 'Luciano' , 'Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike' , 'Sequ3l' , 'Loopers' , 'Julian jordan' , 'Oliver Huntermann' , 'Priyanjana' , 'Siana Catherine' , 'Laughing Buddha' etc.
